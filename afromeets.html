Proposal for Mobile App Development: AfroMeets

Client: AfroMeets.com

Project: Development of a Cross-Platform Dating App

Prepared by: Scott Lee, KimuraTrap Studios LLC

Project Overview

This proposal outlines the plan for developing a cross-platform dating app for AfroMeets.com, incorporating a fresh design, a robust back-end system, a subscription service, and a fully functional front-end. The app will allow users to seamlessly connect, engage, and interact with a streamlined user experience across mobile platforms.

Scope of Work

  • Design & User Interface: A fresh, modern, and mobile-optimized design for a smooth user experience. Branding and UI/UX enhancements to match AfroMeets' identity.
  • Database Setup: A secure, scalable database to manage user profiles, messages, subscriptions, and interactions. Efficient back-end for smooth data operations and integrations.
  • Front-End Development: Cross-platform mobile app development for iOS and Android using a hybrid framework. Responsive design ensuring smooth performance on different devices.
  • Subscription Service: Integration of subscription models to offer premium features. Secure payment gateway integration.
  • Testing & Quality Assurance: Comprehensive testing across all devices and platforms. Bug fixes, performance enhancements, and final adjustments based on client feedback.

Project Team

  • 1 Full-Time Developer: Responsible for back-end development, database setup, and complex functionality like the subscription service.
  • 2 Part-Time Developers: Focus on front-end development, UI/UX design integration, and secondary tasks such as testing and client feedback revisions.

Project Timeline

  • Development Phase: 9 weeks (approximately 2.25 months) for core functionality development, database setup, front-end, and back-end integration.
  • Bug Fixes & Adjustments: 1 month for testing, bug fixes, performance tweaks, and client feedback integration.
  • Total Project Duration: 4 months

Project Cost

The total cost for this project will be $75,000. This includes:

  • 700 total development hours.
  • A dedicated team consisting of:
    • 1 Full-Time Developer (8 hours/day)
    • 2 Part-Time Developers (4 hours/day)
  • The cost includes all design, development, database setup, integration, testing, and bug fixes during the 4-month period.

Payment Terms:

  • Phase 1 (Deposit): 30% or $22,500 upon project initiation. *Non-Refunadable*
  • Phase 2 (Midway): 40% or $30,000 upon completion of the development phase (after 9 weeks).
  • Phase 3 (Final Payment): 30% or $22,500 upon project completion and final delivery.

Additional Notes

  • This proposal is based on a fixed scope, but should additional features or changes be required during the development process, we can discuss an adjusted timeline and budget.
  • The development team will ensure clear and open communication throughout the project to ensure the product aligns with your vision.
  • We will conduct frequent milestones and feedback sessions to ensure the project stays on track.
